Our Story

Scraptel was born from a simple observation: millions of tons of valuable materials end up in landfills every year, while people struggle to find convenient and fair ways to recycle their scrap.

Founded in 2020 by a team of environmental engineers and technology enthusiasts, we set out to bridge this gap. Our founders had worked in traditional recycling for years and saw how technology could revolutionize the industry.

Today, we've grown from a small local operation to a platform serving thousands of customers across the region. We've maintained our core commitment to fair pricing, excellent service, and environmental stewardship.

Every pickup we make, every transaction we facilitate, and every ton we recycle brings us closer to our vision of a truly circular economy where waste becomes wealth for everyone.
